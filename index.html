About the Film

"The Creator" is a 2023 science fiction film directed by Gareth Edwards that explores a future where AI has gained sentience. The film portrays a world divided on AI acceptance, with Asia embracing it while the United States bans it due to perceived threats.

Through its narrative, the film raises important questions about the ethics of AI development, the automation of warfare, and the implications for human privacy and identity.
